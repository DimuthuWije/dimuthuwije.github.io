## Small-Scale Wind Turbine Prototype

This project involved the design, fabrication, and evaluation of a small-scale wind turbine prototype as part of a team-based engineering design project. The system demonstrated mechanical-to-electrical energy conversion through a functional prototype capable of generating electrical output to power a small bulb.

## The Design

The prototype was developed through a complete engineering design process, including conceptual planning, fabrication, assembly, and performance evaluation. The design incorporated a custom blade system and mechanical structure to convert wind energy into rotational motion, which was then converted into electrical output during testing.

## Functional Demonstration

The completed prototype was evaluated through live testing, where the rotating turbine generated electrical output to illuminate a small bulb, demonstrating successful conversion from mechanical wind energy to electrical energy.
